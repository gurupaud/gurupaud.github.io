12. 1 menit = 60 detik

120 liter/menit

= 120/60 liter/detik

= 2 liter/detik

13. 100 m + 100 dam

= (100 ÷ 1000) km + (100 ÷ 100) km

= 0,1 km + 1 km

= 1,1 km
