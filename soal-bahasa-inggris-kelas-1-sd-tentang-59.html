Penjelasan:

Note:

Yang atas versi BritishYang bawah versi Amerika

Penjelasan:

TELLING TIMES

Terdapat dua cara untuk menyatakan waktu dalam bahasa Inggris.  

American (Jam + Menit)  

04:10 ➔ It's four ten 05:25 ➔ It's five twenty-five 01:09 ➔ It's one o-nine (dibaca o nine) 02:43 ➔ It's two forty-three 07:30 ➔ It's seven thirty

British (Menit + Past/To + Jam)  

07:40 ➔ It's twenty to eight 11:25 ➔ It's twenty-five past eleven 04:53 ➔ It's seven to five 08:28 ➔ It's twenty-eight past eight 05:15 ➔ It's (a) quarter past five 09:45 ➔ It's (a) quarter to ten 10:30 ➔ It's half past ten

Note:  

Menit 1-29 gunakan past (menit + past + jam berada) Menit 31-59 gunakan to (sisa menit + to + jam menuju) Menit ke 15 gunakan (a) quarter past diikuti jam berada Menit ke 45 gunakan (a) quarter to diikuti jam menuju Menit ke 30 gunakan half past diikuti jam berada

O'clock

Gunakan o'clock jika tidak ada menit.  

02:00 ➔ It's two o'clock 07:00 ➔ It's seven o'clock

1. What is Taj Mahal actually?

Taj Mahal is actually a mausoleum.

2. What impression do you get when you read the word majestically?

3. Why did the king construct Taj Mahal?

The king constructed Taj Mahal in the memory of his beloved wife and queen.

4. What does the phrase ‘the crown of the palace’ imply?

The phrase 'The Crown of the Palace' implies the name of Shah Jahan’s wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

5. Read the third line of paragraph two. What impression did you get after reading the description?

The third line of the paragraph two 'The white marble is inlaid with semi-precious stones (including jade, crystal, lapis lazuli, amethyst and turquoise) that form the intricate designs'.

After reading the description, I get impression that Taj Mahal is very expensive and worth building.

6. How are the materials and architectural design of Taj Mahal?

The materials and architectural design of Taj Mahal are the finest architectural and artistic achievement.

7. What do all the materials and the architecture indicate?

All the materials and the architecture indicate that Taj Mahal deserves to be one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

8. Where was the queen actually buried?

The queen actually buried at garden level

9. When do you think is the best time to see Taj Mahal. Why do you think so?

I think the best time to see Taj Mahal is at daytime.

Because when the sky is bright and clear, the Taj looks milky white.

10. What do you think about the inclusion of Taj Mahal as one of the Seven Wonders of the World?

I think Taj Mahal is deserved to be one of the Seven Wonders of the World with it is beauty.

11. What is the writer’s purpose in writing the essay?

The writer's purpose in writing the essay to describe about the beauty of Taj Mahal.

12. How does the writer organize his idea?

The writer organize his idea with an openingparagraph that introduces the object going to be described. And then, a series of paragraphs follow the opening to describe the parts or the features or the specific characteristics of the subject

13. What reaction from readers does the writer expect?

The writer expects to help readers visualize what Taj Mahal is like.

14. Read text 1 again. Find out the similarities between text 1(Tanjung Puting National Park)} and text 2 (Taj Mahal)

The similarities between Tajung Puting National Park and Taj Mahal are

- Both of the texts describe about a particular place.

- The purpose of the texts is describe the particular place by describing its orits specific features to help readers visualize what the places are like.

- Both of the texts start with an opening paragraph. In the paragraph there is a topic sentence that introduces the object going to be described. A series of paragraphs follow the opening to describe the parts or the features or the specific characteristics of the subject.

38. kunci jawaban tema 6 kelas 1 sd halaman 59


Kunci jawaban tema 6 kelas 1 sd halaman 59 adalah mengenai kalimat ajakan dan kalimat tanggapan  pada percakapan Udin dan Beni dan di tuliskan di tempat tersedia.  

Pembahasan

Berdasarkan soal yang adik ajukan pada buku tema 6 kelas 1 sd halaman 59 adalah tentang percakapan Udin dan Beni, dimana berdasarkan percakapan tersebut tuliskan kalimat ajakan dan kalimat tanggapannya.  

Kalimat ajakan merupakan yang bersifat mengajakan seseorang untuk melakukan permintaan si pengajak secara bersama-sama.

Kalimat tanggapan merupakan kalimat respon atau sanggahan dari seseorang berdasarkan apa yang di dengar , di lihat, atau di baca pada suatu kejadian atau peristiwa dan lawan bicara.

Pelajari juga tentang contoh kalimat tanggapan, baca di https://brainly.co.id/tugas/2018198  

Pelajari juga tentang contoh kalimat ajakan, baca di https://brainly.co.id/tugas/6297255  

Berikut percakapan Udin dan Beni :

Beni :  Udara sangat sejuk. Mari kita bermain bola di taman.  

Udin :  Maaf, aku tidak bisa. Kakiku sedang sakit.  

Beni :  Oh, maaf, aku tidak tahu. Mari kita bermain yang lain saja.  

Udin :  Ide yang bagus. Kamu teman yang baik.  

Beni :  Ayo, kita ajak teman-teman yang lain.  

Udin :  Ayo. Aku sangat suka.

Jawab :  

Kalimat ajakan :

1. Mari kita bermain bola di taman

2. Mari kita bermain yang lain saja

3. Ayo, kita ajak teman-teman yang lain.

Kalimat tanggapan :  

1. Maaf, aku tidak bisa, Kakiku sedang sakit.

2. Ide yang bagus. Kamu teman yang baik.

3. Ayo. Aku sangat suka.
