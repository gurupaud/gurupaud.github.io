Delivering children on the first day of school is usually the most awaited routine for parents. However, the hectic first day of school, which usually occurs, was not felt in the new school year 2020/2021 which took effect Monday, July 13, 2020.

Competing for a seat, as well as meeting face to face with new friends, may not be felt by students who are entering school for the first time at each level. This is because the introduction to the school environment (MPLS) in the new school year is mostly done online.

Some areas with the status of the Covid-19 green zone have not been able to freely hold the student orientation period. They are obliged to carry out disciplined health protocols.

The Ministry of Education and Culture (Kemendikbud) has only granted permission to enter schools face-to-face for 104 districts / cities throughout Indonesia which are included in the green zone in the Covid-19 distribution map. Meanwhile, other districts / cities that are still included in the yellow and red zones must apply learning through the online system from home. and thank God now the pandemic has reduced and in some areas it is permissible for PTM to be in school but still always use a mask, keep a distance, use a hensenitaizer, even every class is usually subjected to shifts such as absenteeism on Monday 1-16, absent days 17-32 are difficult indeed, do not recognize friends, know also only online only
