how to make tea:

( cara membuat teh )

1. boil water until it boils

(rebus air hingga mendidih)

2. prepare a cup

( siapkan cangkir)

3. put tea bag and 2 tablespoons of sugar in the cup

( masukkan kantong teh dan 2 sendok makan gula kedalam cangkir)

4. put half hot water and half cold water into the cup then stir with a spoon

( masukkan setengah air panas dan setengah air dingin kedalam cangkir lalu aduk dengan sendok)

then the tea is ready to drink..
