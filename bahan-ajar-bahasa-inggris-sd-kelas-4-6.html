Simple Past Tense:

1. Did the baby drink that milk? Yes, he did.

2. Did the girl play badminton? No, She did not.

3. Did the children sleep at ten o'clock? Yes, the children did.

4. Did the film begin at 6 p.m.? No, the film did not.

5. Did father read newspaper? No, father did not.

6. Did Ms.Ivah write a letter? Yes, she did.

7. Did Mr.Nasrul make some kites? No, Mr.Nasrul did not.

8. Did the teacher give some homework? Yes, the teacher did.

9. Did Fitri and Rani send a parcel? No, They did not.

10. Did bik Inah sweep the yard? Yes, she did.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Interrogative dari Simple Past Tense:

(?) Did + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

Yes, Subject + Did.

No, Subject + Did + Not.
